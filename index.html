Story

Once upon a time in Mexico…

Pepe Peligroso, an inconspicuous, yet courageous man from Chihuahua, was sent to the desert to face a dangerous enemy who has been threatening his village for ages.

A final clash with the gigantic rooster, known as „El Pollo Loco“, as tall as a cactus and with claws as big as shovels, will decide the fate of Pepe and the people of Chihuahua.

Will Pepe finally save his village from the dangerous claws of „El Pollo Loco“ and turn into a hero? Let's play...
